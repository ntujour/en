Research Areas

Prof. Hsieh's research bridges computational methods and journalism studies. His work spans online community analysis, social network analysis, quantified-self and lifelogging, eye-tracking in media consumption, digital games and society, and social simulation. He leads the Computational Communication Lab (C.C.Lab) at NTU.

Courses Taught

  • Data Analysis in Journalism
  • Web Design for Storytelling
  • Social Media and Public Opinion Analysis
  • Computational Methods for Communication Research
