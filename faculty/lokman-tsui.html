Research Areas

Dr. Tsui is a leading scholar on internet freedom, digital rights, and authoritarian information control. His research examines internet censorship, digital security, press freedom, and the dynamics of authoritarianism in the digital age — with a particular focus on China, Hong Kong, and broader Asia-Pacific contexts. He has collaborated with The Citizen Lab at the University of Toronto and brings both legal and communication perspectives to his interdisciplinary work.
