Lih-Yun Lin (林麗雲)

Professor

lihyunlin2@gmail.com

Education

  • Ph.D. in Communication, University of Westminster, UK
  • M.A. in Journalism, National Chengchi University (NCCU), Taiwan

Research Areas

Prof. Lin's scholarship centers on the political economy of media, with particular attention to Taiwan's media industry history, public service broadcasting, and the dynamics of press freedom in East Asian contexts. Her work applies critical discourse analysis and qualitative research methodologies to examine media regulation, television systems, and the relationship between media capital and democratic governance.

Courses Taught

  • Communication Theories

Selected Publications

  • Over 25 peer-reviewed journal articles on media regulation, television systems, and press freedom in Taiwan and comparative contexts

  • Numerous book chapters in edited volumes on communication policy and public service media
