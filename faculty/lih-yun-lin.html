Research Areas

Prof. Lin's scholarship centers on the political economy of media, with particular attention to Taiwan's media industry history, public service broadcasting, and the dynamics of press freedom in East Asian contexts. Her work applies critical discourse analysis and qualitative research methodologies to examine media regulation, television systems, and the relationship between media capital and democratic governance.
