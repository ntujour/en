Research Areas

Prof. Rauchfleisch is an internationally recognized scholar in computational communication and political communication. His research examines social media dynamics, journalism studies, disinformation, and social network analysis — with a particular focus on Taiwan's digital public sphere and cross-national comparative studies. He employs large-scale computational methods to study online political discourse, information flows, and media ecosystems.
