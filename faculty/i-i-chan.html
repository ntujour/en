Professional Background

Prof. Chan brings more than three decades of frontline television journalism experience to the classroom. Before joining NTU in 2025, she held senior leadership roles at TVBS — one of Taiwan's leading cable news networks — most recently as Deputy General Manager of the News Department. Her career spans editorial direction, documentary production, and public affairs reporting, making her one of Taiwan's most accomplished broadcast journalists.
