Education

Research Areas

Dr. Tsai's research sits at the intersection of media sociology, cultural studies, and digital media. Her work investigates new media and civic engagement, the platformisation of communication in everyday life, public service media, and the democratisation of mass culture. She brings critical and qualitative perspectives to understanding how digital platforms reshape public participation and cultural production in contemporary Taiwan.
